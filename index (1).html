Suronix Venture – Biomass Pellet Manufacturing

Our biomass pellet manufacturing company is dedicated to producing eco-friendly fuel alternatives that support clean energy goals and reduce dependency on fossil fuels. Using agricultural waste, sawdust, and organic byproducts, we create high-efficiency biomass pellets ideal for industrial, commercial, and domestic heating applications. We are proud to be part of India’s green energy movement—offering scalable solutions for a more sustainable future.
